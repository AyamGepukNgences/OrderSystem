Daily Workflow

Start each shift the same way every time.

This page records attendance, break timing, and the payroll data used by the staff dashboard. Use the same staff profile you use everywhere else in the restaurant system.

1. Choose your staff profile

Select your name first so the system can load your role, location, and shift settings before clocking you in.

2. Clock in, then track breaks properly

Break start and end times are saved separately, so attendance reports and net working hours stay accurate.

3. Clock out when the shift ends

Your daily record is used by management for punctuality checks, hours summaries, and estimated payroll calculations.

Shift start, end, and grace-period rules come from Staff Management. If your schedule looks wrong here, update it there instead of creating a duplicate profile.
